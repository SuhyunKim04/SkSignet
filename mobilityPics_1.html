Our Impact

Annual Forestation Impact of 5,265 Hectares Driven by Charging Innovation

SK Signet's ultra-fast chargers deployed in the US in 2022 achieved a remarkable reduction of 55 thousand tons of carbon, equivalent to creating a forest spanning 5,265 hectares. With 2,273 ultra-fast chargers installed in the US as of December 2022, each charger contributes to an annual carbon reduction of 24.09 tons. In total, SK Signet's chargers have led to a significant reduction of 54,757 tons of carbon emissions.

Social Values

Why

SK Signet's Commitment to Sustainable Growth

SK Signet embarked on its ESG journey with a vision for the sustainable happiness of society. Our goal is to lead the way in achieving carbon neutrality and promote the adoption of eco-friendly energy infrastructure, contributing to sustainable growth.

As a proud member of the SK Group, we are dedicated to driving economic, social, and environmental progress through a spirit of challenge and trust. Furthermore, we aim to redefine our corporate purpose and collaborate with valued partners to create a happier society together.

How

Method 1

Double Bottom Line (DBL)

SK views social enterprises as highly specialized and effective organizations for solving social problems. We believe that for-profit companies can play an integral role in supporting the social enterprise ecosystem to drive up the sustainability of these entities and to ensure they continue to maximize the generation of social value.

Method 2

Double Bottom Line (DBL)

SK believes that it must be superseded by a business model that is articulated around the joint generation of financial returns and social impact. The idea behind this forward-thinking model is that both of these objectives are mutually reinforcing and can be grown both simultaneously and steadily. SK's Double Bottom Line embodies this approach.

Method 3

Shared Infrastructure

In an effort to maximize social value, SK is challenging the assumptions behind modern business models. We are experimenting with shared infrastructure models with a range of external stakeholders to generate innovative solutions that better address clearly identified social and business needs.

Method 4

Social Value Community

SK recognizes the importance of making a collective impact and mobilizing the resources and competencies of its people and external stakeholders to address complex social needs. Recent efforts include the establishment of a fully fledged social value community to increase social awareness and maximize social value output.
